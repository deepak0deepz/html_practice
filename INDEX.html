🌱 Welcome to Green Bridge 🌱



Connecting Farmers and Buyers
Without Middlemen

Green Bridge is an agriculture platform that connects farmers directly with buyers.
Farmers can showcase their crops and buyers can purchase fresh produce
with full transparency and fair pricing.

Platform Features

  • 🌾 Farmers upload crop details
  • 🛒 Buyers browse available crops
  • 💰 Direct buying without brokers
  • 📊 Transparent crop health information
